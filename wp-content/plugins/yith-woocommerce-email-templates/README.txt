YITH WooCommerce Email Templates allows you to create and manage unlimited custom email templates for WooCommerce. 


== Description ==

Display your emails how you want!

The more you customize, the more your users will be satisfied. Create unlimited email templates and customize them with the most suitable logos and colors for your site. All the email your users will receive (new order, cancelled order, completed order, invoice, new account, note, processing order, reset password) will show the personality of your site catching your customers' eye!

= Features: =

* A specific panel to create unlimited email templates. Options let to set: logo, base color, body color, background color and text color.
* A dropdown menu to choose the email template to use.
* An uploader to choose your default logo for templates.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `YITH WooCommerce Email Tempates` from Plugins page

YITH WooCommerce Email Tempates will add a new submenu called "Email Tempates" under "YIT Plugins" menu. Here you are able to configure all the plugin settings.
